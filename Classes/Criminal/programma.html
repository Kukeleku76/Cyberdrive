Class Features:
Hit points:
Hit Dice: 1d8 per Criminal Level
Hit points at 1st level: 8 + your Constitution modifier
Hit points at higher levels: 1d8 (or 5) + your Constitution modifier per Criminal level after the 1st

Proficiencies:
Armour: Light armour
Weapons: Any sort of silenced pistol, any sort of sniper, simple melee weapons
Tools: Choose an Archetype to get tools
Vehicles: Any sort of starship

Saving Throws: Dexterity, Intelligence
Skills: Choose 4 from Acrobatics, Athletics, Deception, Persuasion, Intimidation, Stealth, Sleight of Hand, Perception

Equipment:
(a) A silenced Kruger pistol (b) or any sniper of 1st level
(a) any simple melee weapon (b) or any silenced pistol
(a) an impostor pack or (b) a hired gun pack
Any sort of Light armour

Sneak attack:
Beginning at 1st level, whenever you make an attack on a creature who is unaware of your location, you deal an extra 1d6 damage at 1st level, 2d6 at 7th level, 3d6 at 13th level, 4d6 at 15th level, 5d6 at 18th level.

Cunning action:
At 2nd level, you can, as a bonus action you can now Dash, Disengage, Hide

Ability Score Improvement:
At 3rd, 10th, 12th and 16th level, you can increase 1 ability score of your choice by 2. You can increase 2 ability scores by 1. As normal, you can’t increase an ability score above 20 using this feature.

Expertise:
At 5th level you choose 2 skill proficiencies or 1 skill proficiency and the skill with your tools (depends on Archetype). Your proficiency bonus is doubled for any ability check that you make that uses either of the chosen proficiencies.
At 11th level, you can choose 2 more of your proficiencies (in skills or with tools) to gain this benefit.

Backstab:
Whenever you hit someone in his back with a melee attack without that person being aware of where you are, you deal an extra 4d6 damage to your attack and the enemy becomes frightened for 1 minute.

Fast feet:
At 19th level your base walking speed doubles and whenever you get hit, you can make a Dexterity saving throw. The save DC on this throw has to be higher than the number on the hit roll of the opponent. If you succeed this roll, you take half the damage and roll 5 feet in any direction away from the enemy. Whenever an enemy makes a critical, fast feet can’t be used.

Unhittable:
At 20th level you gain the ability to, whenever an enemy hits you, to make the attack not hit. Your GM first has to roll and tell you the damage before you use this feature. You can only use this features 2 times per long rest.

Criminal Archetypes:
Thieves:
Extra Proficiencies:
Tools: You gain proficiency with the thieves’ tools

Extra Equipment:
Tools: You gain the Thieves’ tools

Fast hands:
At 3rd level, you can also make a Sleight of Hand check, use an item, use a weapon in your Cunning action.

Second-story work:
At 3rd level, you gain the ability to climb faster than normal; climbing doesn’t cost any extra movement anymore.
Whenever you make a running jump, the distance you cover increases by a number of feet equal to your Dexterity modifier.

Silent work:
At 9th level, you gain advantage on any skill related to Dexterity and your proficiency bonus on the Stealth skill doubles.

Disrupter:
At 14th level, you can use simple disruption glitches to enemies.

Thief’s reflexes:
At 17th level, you can take 2 turns on the 1st round of combat. Your first turn is by your normal initiative, the 2nd one is by your normal initiative minus 10. You can only do this in the first round of combat.

Hacker:
Extra Proficiencies:
Tools: Hacker’s tools, bugs
Vehicles: You gain proficiency with every sort of vehicle.

Extra Equipment:
Tools: Hacker’s tools, 3 Mainframe bugs

Hacker, destroyer of doors:
At 3rd level, you gain the ability to open any door with a simple lock system. Lock systems are further explained in the book.

Technologic genius:
You automatically gain proficiency in the Tech saving throw and hacking skill

Engineering? Yes I am also good at that:
As a hacker you are very creative and smart in terms of buildings technological items. This means that whenever you build something, you can throw with advantage on any throw that you need to do, to build that item.

Fast typers:
At 9th level, you can add twice your proficiency bonus to all checks related to the Tech skill.

Safe and Secure:
At 14th level, you can now hack into mainframes from buildings from a distance to 5 times your Criminal level in miles. This way you can safely break into mainframes without needing to get into the building and literally plant a bug in the mainframe.

Expert hacker:
At 17th level, you can open any door, use any disruption system on any sort of target (other rules with races go with this to) and you can use any sort of glitch of the level glitches you can use (with level 17 that are all till level 15 glitches).
