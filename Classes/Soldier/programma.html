Class Features:
Hit Points:
Hit Dice: 1d10 per Soldier level
Hit points at 1st level: 10 + your Constitution Modifier
Hit points at higher levels: 1d10 (or 6) + your Constitution Modifier per Soldier level after the 1st

Proficiencies:
Armour: You are proficient with all medium armour
Weapons: Energy rifles, energy snipers, energy shotguns, energy pistols (heavy and light)
Tools: None
Vehicles: You have proficiency with any sort of land cruiser

Saving Throws: Dexterity, Constitution
Skills: Choose 2 from: Acrobatics, Athletics, Insight, Investigation, Perception, Stealth, Survival, Hacking

Equipment:
(a) A Energy one-shot rifle, (b) or an energy sniper
(a) A energy shotgun, (b) or a heavy pistol
(a) A survivalist’s pack (b) or a hired-gun pack
Any sort of medium armour
4 energy cells filled with 30 energy bullets each

Fighting Style:
At 1st level you can choose a fighting style that suits your character. You can only choose a option once, which means that, when you switch your fighting style, you can’t switch back to your old one.

Rifles
Whenever you try to hit someone with a rifle, you can add an extra +2 to hit over the other modifiers, this increases to a +3 at 4th level, a +4 at 9th level, a +6 at 14th level and a +8 at 19th level

Snipers
Choosing this Fighting Style, you gain the feature Hold Breath. Whenever you hold your breath while aiming, you can add an extra +4 to hit over other modifiers and the sniper deals an extra 1d6 damage to the target, when further than 25 feet away from the target, you can roll with Advantage, if you are 25 feet or closer, you have to roll with Disadvantage no matter what. It takes 1 bonus action to activate Hold Breath, and it has to recharge for 1 turn.

Duel Pistols
With the Style Duel Pistols, you gain the ability that whenever you make a roll to hit with your duel pistols, it counts for both, this also means that when you roll a Critical, both guns shoot a critical.

Shotgun
The Shotgun Fighting Style gains you the ability that your range of your shotgun is increased by 10. This means that with a shotgun which has a range of 10/25, now has a range 20/35. The shotgun Style also gains you 2d6 extra piercing damage to your normal attack, except for when you are out of your range, this means the damage doesn’t go on, when you have to throw with Disadvantage

Ability Score Improvement:
When you reach 2nd level, and again at 5th , 10th , 15th and 20th level, you can increase one ability score of your choice by 2, or you can increase two ability scores of your choice by 1. As normal, you can’t increase an ability score above 20 using this feature.

Improved Instincts:
You use Improved Instincts. For 2 turns, you have advantage on all Saving Throws, hit rolls and skill checks. Your damage dice also doubles. It can be turned on using a bonus action and you can only use it 2 times per long rest. This increase to 3 turns and times per long rest at 11th level, to 4 turns and times per day at 18th level. When you roll a critical on the hit roll, the doubled dice doubles again.

Bonus Attack:
Beginning at 8th level, you gain the ability to use an item or weapon as a bonus action.

Extra Shot:
Beginning at 12th level, your gun shoots 1 extra shot. This shot deals the same amount of damage as a normal shot and you don’t need to roll for it to hit. At 17th level, your guns now shoots 2 extra shots.
