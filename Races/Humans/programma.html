Humans are the race that is good in all sorts of things. If it comes from learning languages or mastering the secret martial arts, Humans can learn both of these in a matter of time.

Full natural: A human unlike most other races, is natural, were the only biological enhancement is the neurochip. This makes it harder for people to hack humans, because of them only having a neurochip and the human intuition.

Class Traits: Ability Score Increase: All of your scores except for Tech increase with 1

Age: Humans age from 0 to 100 were they become adult at the age of 18

Alignment: Most humans are neutral people, listening to the law, but also not. Humans go around from True Neutral to Chaotic Good

Size: A normal human is between 5 and 6 feet tall

Speed: Your base walking speed is 30ft

Human Reaction: You have advantage on all instinctual saving throw
(instinctual throws are throws with DEX, STR, WIS, CHR)

Languages: You can speak read and write common and 1 extra language of your choice.

A wide variety: With their penchant for migration and conquest, humans are more physically diverse than other common races. There is no typical human. An individual can stand from 5 feet to a little over 6 feet tall and weigh from 125 to 250 pounds. Human skin shades range from nearly black to very pale, and hair colours from black to blond (curly, kinky, or straight); males might sport facial hair that is sparse or thick.

Hacking Disadvantage: Because humans only have a neurochip, every enemy has to roll Disadvantage with any hacking or disruption ability to a human.
