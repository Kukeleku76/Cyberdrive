Robots are creatures that are made by other beings. Robots are strong flexibel and intelligent creatures.

Platinum beauties:
Most robots have a platinum skin colour, with red eyes. Most robots weigh about 60 to 80 lbs and most of the time size from 5 to 8 feet tall and have no hair.

Class Features:
Ability score increase: Your Strength, Constitution and Tech scores increase by 1

Age: Robots age from the age of 0 to the maximum age of 500

Alignment: Most robots are Chaotic Neutral, Robots with the WarStar background are Chaotic Evil.

Size: Most robots are sized from 5 to 8 ft.

Speed: 40 ft

Languages: You can speak, read and write Common and 1 extra language of your choice, you can read and write Code.

Mods: As a robot you can choose 1 of these 3 mods:
1: Intelligence mod
The intelligence mod is a mod itself. This mod is called the teacher. The teacher allows you to understand every foreign language and every answer to a question. The teacher is a being itself. Your stats donâ€™t increase, or have advantage, but you can ask the teacher for help with things.

2: Armour mod
The armour mod is a mod which allows your body to identify itself as heavy ring mail armour without disadvantage on stealth. Your normal AC without modifier than is 14.

3: Resurrection mod
This mod gives you the ability to whenever your hit points drop to 0 gain 1 hit point to not have to make Death Saving Throws.
