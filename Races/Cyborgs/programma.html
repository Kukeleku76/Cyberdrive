Cyborgs

Cyborgs Visit the Home page Here!
Cyborgs are enhanced Humans that can easilier do things than Humans, but their social skills aren't that good.

Distrusted Humans:

Cyborgs, because of their enhancements and their loss of being human, are most of the time less trusted by other races, were the humans trust them the least. Most of the time robotic humans are more socialized to other robotic humans or robots.

A wide variety: See page 5 (human)

Class Features:
Ability score increase: All of your scores except for Wisdom and Intelligence increase by 1.

Age: Robotic humans age to maximally 150 were they reach adulthood at the age of 18.

Alignment: As a Cyborg you are Chaotic Neutral.

Size: A normal Cyborg sizes from 5 to 7 feet.

Speed: When normal legs: 30ft, when robotic legs, 40ft.

Languages: You can speak, read and write Common and can read and type Code.

Human Reaction: You have advantage on all instinctual saving throws
(instinctual throws are throws with DEX, STR, WIS)

Robotic Enhancements: You can choose 2 of these robotic enhancements of your choice
1: Legs
Legs give you more walking places and the ability to kick.
The base walking speed with robotic legs is 40ft.
The kick ability:
1d4 + STR modifier/slashing damage at 1st leg level, walking speed = 40ft
1d6 + STR modifier/slashing damage at 5th leg level, walking speed = 45ft
1d8 + STR modifier/slashing damage at 10th leg level, walking speed = 50ft
1d10 + STR modifier/slashing damage at 20th leg level, walking speed = 50ft
All the attacks have a range of 5 ft

2: Hands
Hands give you Advantage every time you grab someone.
At level 5:
Gives you Advantage on every melee attack in a 5 feet range of a target

3: Arm spikes
Arm spikes give you 2 extra weapons were you can use 1 per turn. It takes 1 full turn to regenerate the arm.
At level 5:
You can use the arm spike as a bonus action.
At level 10:
You can use both arm spikes as a bonus action at the same time.
The arm:
1d6 + STR modifier/bludgeoning damage, 5 ft range.

4: Grenade shooter
A grenade shooter gives you an option to shoot a grenade into the battle. It takes 3 full turns to reload the grenade shooter.
The grenade shooter:
1d8/explosion damage at 1st grenade shooter level
1d10/explosion damage at 10th grenade shooter level
1d12/explosion damage at 20th grenade shooter level
All of these attacks have a range of 30 ft, were after the 30 ft you have disadvantage on the hitting throw.
The damage range of the grenade is 25 ft, were both enemies and allies in range have to make a DC 14 or higher saving throw
If they succeed, they take half damage
If they fail, they take full damage

5: Hacking device
A hacking device gives you the ability to instantly hack into simple/intermediate systems.
Simple system:
Unlocking system, doors, garage doors
Elevator system, elevators, platforms

Intermediate system (can only be used for people with neurochips):
Disruption system, radio interference, automatic night vision
Disruption of reality: Making yourself bigger, changing the world around you.

6: Shocker
What a shocker does is, as a reaction to an attack from an opponent within 5 feet, the shocker reacts by shocking them. It takes 1 full turn for the shocker to recharge.

The shocker:
1d4/electrocution damage at 1st shocker level
1d6/electrocution damage at 5th shocker level
1d8/electrocution damage at 10th shocker level
