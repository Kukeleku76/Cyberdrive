Joutirs Visit the Home Page here!
Joutirs are strong, but dumb creatures that are praised for their strength, but couldn't even pass a 1st grader's test. Joutirs are amazingly strong and have thick skin, which allows them to live in any sort of environment.

Distrust in intellect:
Joutirs are distrusted in their intellect. You will never see a Joutir doing a bank robbery. They are just too dumb to do this. Many other races distrust them because of their stupidity. Humans have tried to better the intellect of Joutirs for ages, but it never worked.

Physical tanks:
Joutirs are physically the strongest of all races. Because of their strength they also are the biggest were the smallest Joutir ever was 6.2 feet tall and weigh about 175 lbs. Most Joutirs have a black to brown skin colour, they have robotic red eyes and they most of the time have long dark hair.

Class Features:
Ability score improvement: Your strength skill increases with 4

Age: Joutirs mostly age from 0 to 400 were they become an adult at the age of 120

Alignment: Because of their brutality and harsh actions, Joutirs are mostly Chaotic Evil

Size: Joutirs size from 6 to 8 ft

Speed: 35 ft

Languages: You can speak read and write Common and Joutirian

Skin:
Because of their thick skin, Joutirs get no effect when it is cold or really warm. Massive lung capacity: Joutirs have such a high lung capacity that they can live without breathing for 3 days straight.
