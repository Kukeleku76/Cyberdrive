Gothors are silent and flexibel creatures, being able to climb tall buildings and withstand extreme conditions.

The name Gothor:
The name Gothor comes from the humans. When the Gothors and the humans first met, the humans called them Gothors, here’s why: Gothors have a black skin, long dark hair over their eyes, their eyes are black, size from 4 to 6 feet tall and weigh between 75 and 90 lbs. Gothors, because of their ancient long tradition gain Titanium piercings when they are born. When given birth, a Gothor’s limbs are usually replaced with robotic limbs, to withstand extreme conditions.

Class Features:
Ability score increase: Your Dexterity skill increases with 2.

Age: Most Gothors age from 0 to 270, were they become adults at the age of 56.

Alignment: Most Gothors are True Neutral, some are Chaotic Neutral, this is the player’s choice.

Size: Most Gothors size from 4 to 6 feet tall.

Speed: Your base walking speed is 35 ft.

Languages: You can speak, read and write Common and Gothoric.

Black Skin:
Because of their black skin, Gothors can rarely be seen in the dark. This means that whenever an enemy makes a perception check on a Gothor, whenever the Gothor is in a dark place, it has to throw with Disadvantage.

Robotic Limbs:
Because of your robotic limbs, your body can withstand extreme temperature, stress and pain. With this you can’t be slown down be snow, water, pain, stress or heat.
