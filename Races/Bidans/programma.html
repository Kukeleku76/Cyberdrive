Bidans are small, weak, but hyper-intteligent beings. Bidans are the first ones to invent the HyperSpace and much much more. Bidans

Small, but clever:
Bidans are small, but really smart. They come from learning new languages in a matter of minutes, to hacking into the intergalactual database within seconds. Bidans have a skin colour of blue, the eyes are most of the time cyan and they have short dark blue hair. Bidans have a robotic heart, because of the crisis on their first planet: Saga. Most Bidans size from 3 to 4 feet an weigh about 60 lbs.

Class features:
Ability score increase: Your intelligence skill increases with 3

Age: Most Bidans age from 0 to 2500 were they become adult at the age of 750

Alignment: Most Bidans are nice, lawful creatures. A Bidan is Lawfully Good.

Size: Most Bidans size from 3 to 4 ft

Speed: Your base walking speed is 25 ft

Languages: You can speak, read and write Common, Bidanian and 1 extra language of your choice, and you can read and write Code.

Subclasses: There are 2 subclasses for a Bidan:
1: Gegorian Bidan
2: Hemodobian Bidan

Gegorian Bidan:
A Gegorian Bidan is a Bidan coming from the planet of Gegora. These Bidans are Lawfully good and most of the time very good with tech

Subclass Features:
Ability score increase: Your Tech skill increases with 1
Builder Lover: All checks that have to do with building something (like Sleight of Hand), you can add twice your proficiency bonus to.
Tool collector: Because of your choice to become a Gegorian Bidan, you have proficiency with every single building tool that is created in Gegora.

Hemodobian Bidan:
Hemodobian Bidans were originally Gegorian criminals, send to another planet to be imprisoned there. This planet named Hemodoba, had such a high radiated ring around it, that whenever a vehicle would pass it, it would light on fire immediately. The Hemodobian Bidan were smart and build a vehicle that could withstand that radiation. After a long time of peace on Gegorian, after 560 years after the Gegorians send the first Bidans to Hemodoba, a war broke out against the 2 of them. That war is still going on after 200 years.
Hemodobian Bidans are also known for their incredible skill of hacking.

Subclass Features:
Ability score increase: Your Tech skill increases with 1
Alignment: Because of their criminal past on Gegora, Hemodobian Bidans are Chaotic Evil
Best hackers in the universe: As a Hemodobian Bidan, you have advantage on every Tech saving throw, gain proficiency with the hack skill, and can add an extra +2 to the hacking skill.
Smart, but evil: Hemodobian Bidans are very clever, but also very evil. Because Hemodobian Bidans don’t really trust other races, they are together in groups. (when you one of these classes: Hacker, Trader, Smuggler. You take place in one of these groups):
1: the hackers:
The hackers is a group with the top tier hackers in the universe. Here to earn your place, you must hack into mainframe buildings, banks and many other things

2: The smugglers:
The smugglers is a group that forms around smuggling illegal things throughout the galaxy. Here to earn your place, you must smuggle the most illegal things through the universe, without getting caught.

3: The traders:
The traders is a group of traders. To earn your place here, you must steal smuggle and sell things to people who don’t notice that item is stolen.
